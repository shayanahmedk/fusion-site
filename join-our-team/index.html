The TeleDirect Story

Over 20 years ago, we started as a small company in a crowded field of competitors. We quickly established TeleDirect as a name to be reckoned with, thanks to our passion for exceptional service, quality outsourced solutions and a total commitment to quality & integrity at all times.

Today, TeleDirect is one of the most trusted names in outsourced excellence. From our world-class inbound & outbound call center services to our highly adaptable, intelligent call center platform and more, TeleDirect provides every customer the same attention to detail and urgency they apply to other areas of the businesses.

The TeleDirect secret? We take the hassle and stress out of managing client communication, call center activities, reservations and more. And it all starts with our talented staff. We’re looking for the best customer service reps, call center agents, engineers and more. Enjoy all the advantages of working at TeleDirect. Work from home, with a flexible schedule that encourages optimal work-life balance.
